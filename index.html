Welcome to Grand Technical

London Speaker Hire & PA System Hire

Grand Technical is a London-Based Speaker Hire company that also offers Lighting, Video, and Laptop Dry-Hire options from our Central London Base (Kings Cross). Whether your event is a small conference for 15+ guests or a theatre-sized conference with 300+ Guests, we have the equipment and the technicians that you’ll be needing.

Our Sound Equipment includes Speakers, Microphones, Microphone Stands (Mic Stands), Cables, DI Boxes, Mixing Desks, and Amplifiers. We are also able to provide technicians as a standalone service too. Our unit is available for pre-arranged collections only, so please be sure to call ahead before visiting us! As well as London Speaker Hire, we also offer the following services.

Live Streaming Services

With Conferences being streamed online more and more with each passing week, why not consider adding a Live Stream to your event? This may allow both guests and speakers who were unable to attend the event to still play an active role. Our webcasting solutions include a Podium for your speakers to stand at (with a minimum of 1 fixed camera angled towards them at all times), and can also include a wider shot if you are planning on having a panel session at any point during the event. You will need to remind guests to ask their questions into the designated handheld microphone, or your remote guests will not be able to hear their questions.

Laptop Hire

Grand Technical has provided laptops for a range of events across the country. Whether you are planning a Conference, Exhibition, Coding Retreat, or Graduate programme, we have the right laptops for the task at hand. We only use the highest specification laptops on the market – from brands such as Dell, Hewlett Packard, and Apple. All of our Laptops are delivered with the full Microsoft Office Suite installed – which of course includes Word, Excel, Powerpoint, OneNote, Sharepoint, Teams, and Outlook.

If you need any other software to be pre-installed onto the laptops, such as Photoshop, Logic, Xcode, or any Final Cut Pro, be sure to let us know and we will endeavour to make this a reality for you and your guests/clientele.

All of our laptops are delivered in smart, damage proof flight cases, and are fully tested one-by-one before being delivered; to ensure they are all in working condition. We are also able to include any amount of extension leads (power), mouse mats, mice, and keyboards where needed.
